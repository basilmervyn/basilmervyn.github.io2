BASIL MERVYN

Everything Can Be Expressed With Just A Little Imagination

GIVE IT LIFE

Ever thought of being so out of the box but there's something keeps on holding you back. Society keeps your wild thoughts caged, torutures you to live a life without fun and imagination. Society brands you with these fears of 'they will point a finger', 'they won't like it', and the major one that killed comedy "they will be offended". This has imprisoned the power of man to be artistic. It has caused the inevitable disaster of human beings being utterly boring creatures. I mean, we've even turned into entertaining ourselves with the driest of jokes.


NO MORE! We are an organization that stands against this brutallity inflicted on Art. We hold art events where everyone can be whoever they want. Where everyone can express themselves with no judgement at all. We aim at bringing colour back to the world. We shall and we will end this black and white era. Support us or just get the fuck outta our webpage.

This was replicated on the second event we had and that built our reputation. This is why we are organizing our third event, not for our sake but for you our people. Let's grow together. Bring back colour to our dull world.
